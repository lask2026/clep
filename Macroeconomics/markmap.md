# CLEP Macroeconomics Complete Mindmap

## Module 1: Basic Economic Concepts (8-12%)

### Scarcity

- Limited resources ≠ unlimited wants

- Forces economic choices

### Opportunity Cost

- Next best alternative foregone

- \*\*Trap\*\*: Only highest valued one

### PPC

- On curve: efficient

- Inside: inefficient

- Outside: unattainable

- \*\*Shift out\*\*: growth

### Comparative Advantage

- Lower opportunity cost

- Terms of trade between OCs

- \*\*Trap\*\*: Not absolute advantage

### Supply \& Demand

- Demand: P↓ → Qd↑

- Supply: P↑ → Qs↑

- Eq: Qd=Qs

- \*\*Ceiling\*\*: shortage

- \*\*Floor\*\*: surplus

## Module 2: Measurement (12-16%)

### GDP

- GDP = C+I+G+(X-M)

- \*\*Trap\*\*: No intermediates/transfers

- Nominal vs real

- Deflator = (Nom/Real)\*100

### Inflation

- Inflation = (I2-I1)/I1 \* 100

### Unemployment

- Rate = (U/LF)\*100

- Frictional/structural/cyclical

- \*\*Natural\*\*: F+S

## Module 3: AD/AS (15-20%)

### Aggregate Demand

- AD = C+I+G+(X-M)

- Downward: wealth/interest/NX effects

### Aggregate Supply

- \*\*SRAS\*\*: upward (sticky wages)

- \*\*LRAS\*\*: vertical (potential GDP)

### Gaps

- Recessionary: Yactual < Ypotential

- Inflationary: Yactual > Ypotential

### Multipliers

- Spend = 1/(1-MPC)

- Tax = -MPC/(1-MPC)

## Module 4: Financial Sector (15-20%)

### Money

- Medium/unit/store of value

- M1/M2

### Banking

- Money mult = 1/rr

### Money Market

- \*\*Nominal i\*\*

- Vertical MS

### Loanable Funds

- \*\*Real i\*\*

### Fed Tools

- Buy bonds → MS↑ → i↓

- Sell → opposite

## Module 5: Inflation/Unemployment/Stabilization (20-25%)

### Phillips Curve

- Short-run: ↓U, ↑π trade-off

- Long-run: vertical at natural rate

### Inflation Types

- \*\*Demand-pull\*\*: AD↑

- \*\*Cost-push\*\*: SRAS←

- \*\*Stagflation\*\*: both ↑

### Policy

- Recession: Exp F/M

- Inflation: Con F/M

### Deficits/Debt

- Deficit = G-T (flow)

- Debt = accumulated deficits

## Module 6: Growth (5-10%)

### Sources

- Human capital

- Physical capital

- Technology

- Institutions

- LRAS → right

## Module 7: Open Economy (9-13%)

### Balance of Payments

- Current + Financial = 0

### Exchange Rates

- Appreciation → NX↓

- Depreciation → NX↑

- High r → capital in → appreciate

- 3. Ceiling/shortage vs floor/surplus
