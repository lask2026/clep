# Human Growth & Development CLEP: Deep Dive

## I. Theoretical Foundations (10%)
### Cognitive Developmental (Jean Piaget)
- **4 Stages**
    - **Sensorimotor (0-2y)**: Object Permanence (8-12m), A-not-B error
    - **Preoperational (2-7y)**: Symbolic thought, Egocentrism (3-mountain task), Centration, Irreversibility
    - **Concrete Operational (7-11y)**: Conservation (Number->Liquid->Volume), Reversibility, Seriation
    - **Formal Operational (12+)**: Abstract thought, Hypothetico-Deductive reasoning, Imaginary Audience
- **Learning Mechanics**: Schema, Assimilation, Accommodation, Equilibration
### Psychosocial (Erik Erikson) - 8 Stages
- **Infancy (0-1y)**: Trust vs. Mistrust
- **Toddler (1-3y)**: Autonomy vs. Shame/Doubt
- **Preschool (3-6y)**: Initiative vs. Guilt
- **School Age (6-12y)**: Industry vs. Inferiority
- **Adolescence (12-18y)**: Identity vs. Role Confusion
- **Early Adult (18-25y)**: Intimacy vs. Isolation
- **Middle Adult (25-65y)**: Generativity vs. Stagnation
- **Late Adult (65+)**: Ego Integrity vs. Despair
### Sociocultural (Lev Vygotsky)
- **ZPD**: Zone of Proximal Development (Gap where learning happens)
- **Scaffolding**: Gradual removal of support
- **Private Speech**: Self-guidance tool
### Ecological Systems (Bronfenbrenner)
- **Microsystem**: Immediate (Family, School)
- **Mesosystem**: Connections (Parent-Teacher)
- **Exosystem**: Indirect (Parent's job)
- **Macrosystem**: Culture/Values
- **Chronosystem**: Time/History

## II. Research Methodology (6%)
### The "Trap" Designs
- **Longitudinal**: **Same** subjects, **repeated** over time (Change)
- **Cross-Sectional**: **Different** ages, **one** point in time (Differences/Cohort effect)
- **Cross-Sequential**: **Multiple** cohorts, **followed** over time
### Experimental vs. Correlational
- **Experiment**: Random assignment + Manipulation = **Causation**
- **Correlation**: Relation only (-1.0 to +1.0); Correlation != Causation
### Variables
- **Independent (IV)**: The Predictor/Cause
- **Dependent (DV)**: The Outcome/Effect
- **Confounding**: Third variable interference

## III. Biological & Physical (18%)
### Prenatal & Teratogens
- **Stages**: Germinal (0-2w), Embryonic (2-8w - Organogenesis), Fetal (8w+)
- **Critical Periods**: Organs most vulnerable during formation
- **Hazards**: FAS (Facial abnormalities), Zika (Microcephaly), Rubella (Heart/Deafness)
### Brain Development
- **Prefrontal Cortex**: Planning/Impulse control (develops until ~25)
- **Limbic System**: Emotion (develops earlier than PFC)
- **Processes**: Myelination (speed), Synaptic Pruning (efficiency)
### Motor Milestones
- **Principles**: Cephalocaudal (Head-to-Toe), Proximodistal (Center-Out)
- **Ages**: Lift head (2m), Roll (4-6m), Sit (6-8m), Walk (12m), Pincer Grasp (9-12m)
### Genetics
- **Reaction Range**: Genes set limits, environment sets position
- **Disorders**: Down (Trisomy 21), PKU (Diet treatable), Sickle Cell

## IV. Cognitive, Language & Intel (26%)
### Language Stages
- **Pre-speech**: Cooing (2-3m), Babbling (4-6m)
- **Words**: Holophrastic (12m), Telegraphic (18-24m)
- **Errors**: Overextension (All 4-legs=dog), Underextension (Only my dog=dog)
- **Theories**: Chomsky (Nativist/Innate), Skinner (Learning/Imitation)
### Intelligence (IQ)
- **Formula**: (MA / CA) x 100
- **Cattell**: Fluid (Logic/Declines) vs. Crystallized (Facts/Stays)
- **Gardner**: 8 Multiple Intelligences
- **Levels of ID**: Mild (55-69), Moderate (40-54), Severe (25-39), Profound (<25)

## V. Social, Emotional & Family (28%)
### Attachment (Ainsworth/Bowlby)
- **Secure (65%)**: Distressed when left, comforted on return
- **Avoidant (20%)**: Ignores parent; no distress
- **Resistant (10-15%)**: Clingy, angry/resists comfort
- **Disorganized (5-10%)**: Fearful, contradictory behavior
### Parenting Styles (Baumrind)
- **Authoritative**: High Demand / High Warmth (Best outcomes)
- **Authoritarian**: High Demand / Low Warmth (Anxious/Obedient)
- **Permissive**: Low Demand / High Warmth (Impulsive)
- **Uninvolved**: Low Demand / Low Warmth (Worst outcomes)
### Peer Play (Parten)
- **Stages**: Solitary -> Onlooker -> Parallel -> Associative -> Cooperative
### Moral & Gender
- **Kohlberg**: Preconventional (Me), Conventional (Rules), Postconventional (Ethics)
- **Gender**: Identity (2-3y), Stability (3-4y), Constancy (4-5y)
